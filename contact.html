Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours. Pro and Enterprise customers receive priority support with faster response times.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes! Phone support is available for Pro and Enterprise customers during business hours (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM PST).

Can I schedule a demo?

Absolutely! Contact our sales team to schedule a personalized demo of OpenWeb Starter's features and capabilities.

What's the best way to get technical help?

For technical issues, our live chat and email support are the fastest options. You can also check our documentation and community forum.

Do you provide training for teams?

Yes! We offer custom training sessions for Enterprise customers and can provide onboarding assistance for teams of all sizes.
