Our Story

OpenWeb Starter was born from a simple observation: too many great ideas never see the light of day because creating a website seemed too complicated or expensive.

In 2023, our founder Sarah was helping a local nonprofit create their website. Despite having amazing programs and passionate volunteers, they struggled to get online because traditional web development was beyond their budget and technical capabilities.

That's when we realized we needed to build something different. Not just another website builder, but a platform that combines the power of professional web development with the simplicity of visual editing.

Today, OpenWeb Starter has helped thousands of individuals and organizations bring their ideas to life online. From personal portfolios to business landing pages, we're proud to be part of their digital journey.
